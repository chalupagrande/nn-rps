Rock Paper Scissors Bot!

Hey! Thanks for taking part in my experiment.
This is a small application is to help train a Neural Net to play Rock Paper Scissors.

In this application, it is important that you play as if you are playing a human. For instance, if the computer plays Paper several times in a row, let that influence your decision on what you should play. You can watch you Win, Loss and Tie percentages change as you play. Try and maximize your win percentage.

For ease of use, you can also use your keyboard to make your selection:
R or LEFT ARROW = Rock,
P or DOWN ARROW = Paper,
S or RIGHT ARROW = Scissors
